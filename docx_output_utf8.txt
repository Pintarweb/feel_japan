MOUNT FUJI CLIMBING 5D4N 
Beginning of June-beginning of Sept
DAY1  NARITA AIRPORT (L/D) (YIDA GUIDE - Eng) 
MEET AND GREET BY TOUR GUIDE AT NARITA AIRPORT LOBBY 
⚫ TRANSFER TO HOTEL 
⚫ STOP AT PREMIUM OUTLET NEAR NARITA AIRPORT / AKIHABARA 
⚫ LUNCH AT AKIHABARA 
⚫ PREPARATION FOR CLIMBING (PURCHASE CLIMBING ACCESSORY) 
⚫ DINNER AT RESTAURANT NEARBY HOTEL 
*(TOUR GUIDE WILL ADVISE WHAT TO BUY / PREPARE FOR BREAKFAST AND LUNCH FOR 
THE NEXT DAY) 
LUNCH AND DINNER AT LOCAL RESTAURANT
DAY2  CLIMBERS BUS TRANSFER TO MOUNT FUJI (B, D-bento) (2BUS + 2GUIDE)
1bus from YIDA transfer shinkansen, 1 bus from climbing team(1 Guide from YIDA Eng, 1 from climbing Team-Mandarin -Japanese)
Pick up and transfer to Tokyo station,
Take shinkansen from Tokyo -Mishima  09:03→09:45
Meet with Climbing Team at Mishima 10:10am
10:10 Meet at the North Exit of Mishima Station and take a bus to Fujinomiya 5th Station.
11:40 Arrive at the 5th Station, organize equipment, and distribute rental supplies.
12:10 Hike to the 6th Station mountain hut (approximately 1 hour for altitude acclimatization) and have lunch at the hut's canteen.
13:10 Begin the climb.
17:00 Arrive at Yamaguchi Sanso (mountain hut).
17:30 Dinner and prepare for the next day's hike.
20:00 Lights out and rest.
Note: The mountain hut is basic, without a bathroom, and has shared sleeping areas. Please bring your own earplugs and eye mask.
DAY 3  Hiking Day: Summiting, sunrise viewing, and descent; check into a hot spring hotel.
01:00 Wake up (time may be slightly adjusted) (BLD-YIDA arrange) (2BUS + 2GUIDE)
(1bus from YIDA drive back Tokyo, 1 bus from climbing team)(1 Guide from YIDA Eng, 1 from climbing Team Mandarin-Japanese)
01:30 Depart for the summit attempt
04:00 Arrive at the summit and enjoy a simple breakfast
04:30–05:00 Enjoy a best view of the sunrise!
08:30 Begin the descent
12:00–14:00 Return to Fujinomiya 5th Station, lunch
15:00 Travel to the Mishima natural hot spring "Fugetsu no Yu" Dominion Mishima for a relaxing soak
18:00 Dinner
Notes: The temperature at the summit is close to 0℃. Please prepare a down jacket, headlamp, gloves, and hat.
After watching the sunrise, descend the mountain promptly to avoid congestion and exhaustion.Protect your knees during the descent; knee braces and trekking poles are recommended.
DAY 4  Check out of Mishima Onsen Hotel, back Tokyo (B / L / D) (BUS + GUIDE)
TOKYO TOUR WITH GUIDE 
BREAKFAST, LUNCH AND DINNER AT LOCAL RESTAURANT
DAY5  AIRPORT TRANSFER (B) (BUS) 
CHECK OUT HOTEL 
TRANSFER TO AIRPORT /END OF SERVICEHOTEL
