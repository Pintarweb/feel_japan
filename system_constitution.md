# Feel Japan with K: B2B System Constitution

## 1. Brand Identity & Design Tokens (The "Zen Luxury" Vibe)
- **Palette:** - Primary: Midnight Navy (`#001A33`) - Symbolizes authority and trust.
  - Accent: Brushed Gold (`#C5A059`) - Used for CTAs and luxury highlights.
  - Background: Soft Off-White (`#F9F9F9`) - Clean, minimalist "gallery" feel.
- **Typography:**
  - Headers: Playfair Display (Serif) - Communicates high-end heritage.
  - Body: Montserrat or Inter (Sans-Serif) - For professional readability.
- **Atmosphere:** Minimalist, high-contrast, and spacious. Avoid "busy" layouts or generic travel icons.

## 2. B2B Functional Rules
- **Audience:** Travel Agents (Wholesale), not end-consumers.
- **Terminologies:** Use "Net Rates," "Pax," "Incentive Travel," and "Land Arrangement."
- **Privacy:** Pricing/Net Rates must always be behind a "Partner Inquiry" or "Login" barrier. Never show wholesale prices on public-facing components.
- **White-Labeling:** Components must support a "Client-Ready Mode" where 'Feel Japan with K' branding is minimized so agents can show it to their clients.
