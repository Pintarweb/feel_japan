The Journey

Day 01

Arrival & Tokyo

(Lunch & Dinner Included)

  • Meet and Greet by Tour Guide at Narita Airport lobby
  • Depart for **Tokyo City Tour**
  • Asakusa Kannon Temple & Nakamise Shopping Street
  • Visit **Asakusa Mosque** for prayers
  • Explore **Akihabara**, **Odaiba** & witness the **Shibuya Crossing**
Day 02

Mt Fuji Excursion

(Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner)

  • Check out and depart for **Mt. Fuji** area
  • Visit **Mt. Fuji 5th Station** (Subject to weather/snow condition)
  • Shopping at **Gotemba Premium Outlet** with Mt. Fuji views
  • Prayer stop at dedicated prayer space in Gotemba
  • Move to **Hamamatsu / Toyohashi** area for overnight stay
Day 03

Kyoto Cultural Tour

(Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner)

  • Check out and depart for **Kyoto City Tour**
  • Visit the iconic **Golden Pavilion** (Kinkaku-ji)
  • Stroll through **Arashiyama Bamboo Forest** & **Togetsu Bridge**
  • Visit **Kyoto Mosque** for prayer time
  • Move to Osaka for check-in
Day 04

Osaka City Tour

(Breakfast/Lunch)

  • Visit **Osaka Castle Park** (Photo stop)
  • Visit **Kobe Mosque** for prayer time
  • Explore **Shinsaibashi & Dotonbori** for shopping and food
  • Dinner by own pax arrangement
Day 05

Departure

(Breakfast)

  • Hotel check-out
  • Private transfer to Airport
  • Flight back home • End of Service
