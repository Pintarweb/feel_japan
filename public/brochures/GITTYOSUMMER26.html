The Journey

Day 01

Arrival & Tokyo City

(Lunch & Dinner Included)

  • Meet and Greet by Tour Guide at Narita Airport lobby
  • Depart for **Tokyo City Tour**
  • Asakusa Kannon Temple & Nakamise Shopping Street
  • Photo stop at **Tokyo Skytree** and visit **Asakusa Mosque**
  • Explore **Ueno Ameyoko Market**, **Akihabara** Hobby Town
Day 02

Mt Fuji Excursion

(Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner)

  • Depart from hotel for **Mount Fuji Excursion**
  • Visit **Mt. Fuji 5th Station** (Subject to weather/snow condition)
  • Shopping at **Gotemba Premium Outlet** (Prayer space available)
  • **Shinkansen (Bullet Train)** Experience from Odawara to Tokyo
  • Group walk back to hotel with guide via local train
Day 03

Tokyo Modern Life

(Breakfast/Lunch)

  • Visit **Harajuku (Takeshita Street)** and **Tokyo Mosque**
  • **Shopping at Halal Market** (Tokyo Mosque area)
  • Witness the **Shibuya Crossing** & visit **Tsukiji Outer Market**
  • Explore **Odaiba** (Rainbow Bridge & Gundam Statue)
  • Dinner by own pax arrangement

Free & Easy

(Breakfast)

  • Recommended: Tokyo Disneyland or Disney Sea experience
  • Lunch and Dinner by own pax

Departure

(Breakfast)

  • Hotel check-out
