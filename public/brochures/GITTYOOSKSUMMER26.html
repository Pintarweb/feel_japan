The Journey

Day 01

Arrival & Tokyo

(Lunch & Dinner Included)

  • Meet and Greet by Tour Guide at Narita Airport lobby
  • Depart from airport for **Tokyo City Tour**
  • Visit **Asakusa Kannon Temple** & **Nakamise Street**
  • Visit **Asakusa Mosque** for prayer time
  • **Akihabara (Hobby Town)** exploration
  • **Odaiba (Rainbow Bridge / Gundam Statue)**
  • Witness the world-famous **Shibuya Crossing**
Day 02

Mt Fuji Excursion

(Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner)

  • Check out hotel and depart for **Mt. Fuji** excursion
  • Visit **Mt. Fuji 5th Station** (Subject to weather condition)
  • Shopping at **Gotemba Premium Outlet** (Prayer space available)
  • Moving to **Hamamatsu / Toyohashi** area for overnight stay
Day 03

Kyoto Cultural Tour

(Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner)

  • Check out and depart for **Kyoto City Tour**
  • Visit the iconic **Golden Pavilion** (Kinkaku-ji)
  • Visit **Kyoto Mosque** for prayer time
  • Explore **Arashiyama Bamboo Forest** & **Togetsu Bridge**
  • Transfer to Osaka for hotel check-in
Day 04

Osaka City Tour

(Breakfast/Lunch)

  • Visit **Osaka Castle Park** (Photo stop)
  • Visit **Kobe Mosque** for prayer time
  • Explore **Shinsaibashi & Dotonbori** districts
  • Dinner by own pax arrangement
Day 05

Departure

(Breakfast)

  • Hotel check-out
  • Private transfer to Airport
  • Flight back home • End of Service
